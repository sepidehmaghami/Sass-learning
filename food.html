    What fruit and vegetables can Cockatiels eat?

  • Apples
  • Apricots
  • Banana
  • Blackberries
  • Cherries (not the pit)
  • Coconut
  • Dates
  • Damsons
  • Figs
  • Grapes (with caution as they have a lot of sugar)
  • Grapefruit
  • Kiwi
  • Melon (all types)
  • Pears
  • Pineapple
  • Plums
  • Pomegranates
  • Peaches
  • Raspberry
  • Mango
  • Melons
  • Nectarines
  • Strawberries

    Vegetables

  • Asparagus
  • Beans (cooked includes lentils, brown rice, quinoa)
  • Brussel sprouts
  • Cabbage all types
  • Carrots and their tops
  • Chinese vegetables (bok choi)
  • Corn (preferred on cob)
  • Courgettes
  • Cucumber
  • Kale
  • Parsnips
  • Peas (especially still in pod)
  • Peppers (red/green & hot)
  • Potatoes (prefer cooked)
  • Pumpkin
  • Spinach (in moderation)
  • Squash
  • Sweet potato and yams (cockatiels prefer cooked

    What can Cockatiels not eat?

  • Alcohol
  • Avocado
  • Chocolate
  • High fat
  • High salt foods
